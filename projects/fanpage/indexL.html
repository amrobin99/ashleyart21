Loco Lucho's Latino Kitchen

PUERTO RICAN | MON-SUN 9AM-6PM

Loco Lucho was founded by Luis Liceaga and Rafi Nieto, two Puerto Rican chefs that relocated to Philadelphia. Both developed a passion for cooking through time spent cooking with their families. Their mission is "to provide Philadelphians and visitors alike the opportunity to taste and experience the delicious flavors of Puerto Rican and Latino street food."
