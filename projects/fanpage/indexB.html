By George!

ITALIAN | MON-SAT 8AM-6PM

By George! opened in 1990 and is owned by the Mickel family. The restaurant specializes in Italian and comfort food with popular items on the menu including cheesesteaks, pizzas, and homemade pasta.

By George! is a unique merchant in Reading Terminal as it's one of the few vendors that also has an international presence. Stemming from their "buy one, feed one" mission, a portion of the money from every cheesesteak purchased is donated to charity and used to feed children in El Salvador on a trip that the Mickels make every year.
