Sweet T's Bakery

DESSERTS | TUE-SAT 11AM-6PM

Sweet T's Bakery, owned by Tia and Mark El, is the first black owned bakery in Reading Terminal. The recipes used for Sweet T's sweet potato based desserts come from Tia's grandmother, whom she would bake with when she was younger. It first started as a brick-and-mortar shop before becoming a food truck and then a vendor in Reading Terminal Market in 2020.
