Hershel's East Side Deli

DELI | MON-SUN 8AM-6PM

In 2000, Stephen Safern opened Hershel's East Side Deli to honor his Eastern European lineage. The food, an assortment of sandwiches and side orders, is made from scratch every day. Hershel, the namesake of the business, grew up in Poland during World War II and immigrated to America in the late 1940s. He worked at Katz's Deli in New York's LES and retired in 1989. Hershel's East Side Deli is therefore an opportunity for his nephew, Stephen, to continue his legacy and share the traditional cuisine he grew up with and loves.
