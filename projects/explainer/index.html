How to Bleach Dye

materials needed

bleach

water

spray bottle

rubber bands

clothing of choice

STEPS

1) Get a sweatshirt, t-shirt, etc.

2) Tie up your sweatshirt with rubber bands

3) Put equal parts bleach and water into a spray bottle

4) Spray the sweatshirt all over

5) Let it sit for 10-20 minutes

6) Take the rubber bands off and rinse!
